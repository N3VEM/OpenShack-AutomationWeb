Allstar node 56001

the N3VEM Repeater is:

Open to ALL ham radio operators according to these prinicipals:

  • The inherent worth and dignity of every person
  • Justice, equity and compassion in human relations
  • Acceptance of one another and encouragement to growth both inside and ouside of our hobby and service
  • The goal of world community with peace, liberty, and justice for all
  • Respect for the interdependent web of existence of which we are a part

On the N3VEM Repeater you will hear:

  • Casual and friendly discussions about whatever interests those using the repeater
  • QSTs and Bulletins of Interest to All Amateurs as permitted by FCC Part 97
    1. AR Newsline: Sundays at 2pm Eastern (via RF only)
  • Conversations occuring on Allstar Node 55915 (Ham Radio Workbench) as the repeater will primarily be connected to that node (expect when transmitting bulletins)
  • International Repeater Tails
    1. Why are their tail messages in foreign languages? Lancaster County is incredibly diverse! The languages you'll hear are all the native languages of folx that live here! Hearing these messages is friendly and inviting to them, and a good reminder for the rest of us of the diversty that exists right here where we live! These messages all equate to essentially 'Everyone is welcome on this repeater'
    2. Currently there are tail messages in: Arabic, Bengali, Chinese, Dari, Filipino, French, German, Hindi, Korean, Nepali, Pashto, Portuguese, Russian, Spanish, Swahili, Urdu, and Vietnamese
